# Terms of Service

**EU261 Claim Agent** — Last updated: May 2026

## Nature of the service

EU261 Claim Agent is an open-source tool that automates the administrative steps involved in filing a claim under EU Regulation 261/2004 (and the equivalent UK retained regulation). It:

1. Collects the information needed to assess a claim.
2. Applies a deterministic rules engine to determine eligibility and the applicable compensation amount.
3. Drafts a formal demand letter to the airline.
4. Submits the claim on your behalf using the airline's publicly accessible claim portal.
5. Escalates unresolved claims to the relevant National Enforcement Body (NEB).

## Not legal advice

**This service does not provide legal advice.** The eligibility assessment is a mechanical application of the regulation's criteria to the facts you provide; it is not a legal opinion. The demand letter is a template draft and not a legal document. You should seek independent legal advice if your claim is disputed or involves complex facts.
